## From Concept to Launch: Full-Cycle Game Development

> A tactical card game where players battle with meme-inspired characters across 8 turns. Highest score wins.

**Meme Card Arena** is a card game based on classic Brazilian and internet memes, published by Radical Dreamers—a small game studio I co-founded. As **game designer**, I owned the entire vision: mechanics, interaction loop, art direction, monetization, and product strategy.

![Meme Card Arena - Card battle featuring meme-inspired characters](/assets/MemeCardArena.png)

### Game Design & Mechanics

The core loop is straightforward but strategic: **place cards on the field to accumulate points** and use **effects to remove the opponent's cards**. The player with the most points after **8 turns** wins. I defined every rule, balance, and interaction pattern to create a fun, competitive experience grounded in meme culture.

### Team & Execution

I managed a **multidisciplinary team of 4 people** (development, art, music, marketing) using **agile methodologies** to ship on schedule. Beyond design, I contributed directly to the codebase using **Unity and C#** and handled **Google Play publication** (the game has since been delisted).

### Scope of Ownership

- **Game mechanics** — Turn structure, scoring, card effects, win conditions
- **Artistic & visual direction** — Character style, card layout, UI/UX patterns
- **Monetization model** — Revenue strategy aligned with the free-to-play model
- **Production** — Sprints, backlog, and coordination across disciplines
