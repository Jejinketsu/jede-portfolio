## Rapid MVP: Quote & Invoice SaaS with Stripe

> End-to-end quote generation, PDF export, and subscription management—delivered in 2 days to validate AI-assisted development velocity.

**OrçaFácil** is a SaaS for creating professional quotes and invoices. I built it in **2 days** as a technical challenge to test my ability to ship a complete product from zero using AI-powered workflows.

### What I Shipped

- **Core feature:** Custom PDF generation for quotes and invoices
- **Stripe integration** for subscription and payment management
- **i18n** with Portuguese (Brazil) and English
- **Next.js & TypeScript** for a scalable, maintainable foundation

### Technical Approach

Despite the tight timeline, the project includes production-ready patterns: subscription handling via Stripe, localized UI and content, and a clean user flow for creating and exporting documents. It demonstrates that disciplined use of modern tooling and AI assistance enables rapid, high-quality delivery without sacrificing code quality.
