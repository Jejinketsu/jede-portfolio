## Enterprise-Grade Data Platform at Scale

> Real-time consumer sentiment analysis, economic trackers, and strategic reports for executives at Google, Amazon, Meta, and leading enterprises worldwide.

**Morning Consult Pro** is a Washington D.C.–based data intelligence platform that synthesizes consumer sentiment across politics, markets, brands, and economics. The platform serves over **1 million active users** with interactive visualizations, trend trackers, and comprehensive reports designed to inform C-suite decision-making.

### Technical Scope & Impact

As a frontend and backend developer, I delivered **20+ production features** across the user-facing application and internal tooling:

- **Next.js & TypeScript** frontend for complex, high-traffic dashboards with interactive charts, date range controls, and real-time data views
- **PHP/WordPress** backend integration for content management and dynamic routing
- **Dynamic redirects** controlled via CRM with sub-**15ms response times** and **$0.26/month infrastructure cost**
- **Paywall strategies** for email capture and churn prevention
- **Navigation control systems** for internal teams to manage site structure and content flow

### Performance & Efficiency

I focused on low infrastructure cost and high performance under heavy traffic. Redirects were optimized for minimal latency and minimal cloud spend, demonstrating that effective solutions don't require expensive architecture. I also reached **top 2** among company developers in AI agent usage for faster, higher-quality delivery.
