## Full-Stack B2B Platform for Automotive Detailing

> End-to-end system bridging PCS Auto Detailing (Massachusetts) and dealerships for service tracking, invoicing, and payment management.

**PCS Auto Detailing** provides premium auto detailing services for major dealerships such as Audi, Dodge Ram, Subaru, and others. I built the entire platform **from scratch as a solo developer** to digitize their B2B workflows.

### System Architecture & Features

- **Next.js & TypeScript** for a modern, type-safe full-stack application
- **Role-based dashboards** for administrators, employees, and dealership representatives
- **Invoice generation and tracking** with status workflows (open, pending, paid)
- **Service tracking** for jobs performed at dealership locations
- **Payment management** with clear KPIs (total invoices, money paid, pending, open amounts)

### Business Impact

Within the first month of operation, the platform generated **over $20,000 in invoices** for client billing. The invoice management module supports granular filtering, search, and status views, enabling both PCS staff and dealership users to access their respective data securely.
