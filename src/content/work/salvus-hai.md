## Healthcare Assistive Intelligence at Scale

> Plan and monitor home care visits, prescriptions, and care plans in real-time across web and mobile.

**Salvus HAI** (Healthcare Assistive Intelligence) is an intelligent cloud platform that enables home care centers to coordinate healthcare professionals, medical visits, and patient care workflows. I contributed to features serving **over 30,000 healthcare professionals**, including:

- **Medication prescription and administration** with dosage tracking and validation
- **Clinical progress tracking** and medical assessments
- **Diagnosis workflows** integrated with care plans
- **Real-time visit scheduling and monitoring**

### Technical Stack & Architecture

The project applied **Clean Architecture** and **Atomic Design** for maintainability at scale. On the mobile side, I used **React Native** with Expo Bare Workflow and Expo Modules, **Styled-Components**, **Redux**, **SQLite** with **TypeORM**, and form libraries such as **Yup**, **Unform**, and **React Hook Form**. On the backend, I worked with **Node.js**, **MySQL**, and **Docker** for deployment and infrastructure.
